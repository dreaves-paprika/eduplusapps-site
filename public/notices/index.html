2. Tax Information Disclaimer

The IRS Annual Report feature in EDU Mileage+ produces a summary of your trip records intended for informational purposes only. It is not tax advice and is not a substitute for professional tax preparation.

You should consult a qualified tax professional regarding the deductibility of unreimbursed business expenses, the applicable mileage rates for your situation, and proper reporting on your tax return. Tax treatment of mileage varies by jurisdiction, employment status, and personal circumstances.

The mileage rates displayed in the App reflect IRS standard mileage rates published at the time each trip was recorded. You are responsible for confirming the correct rate for your specific situation, including any mid-year rate changes that may have been issued after a trip was logged.

Under IRS regulations, taxpayers are generally required to retain supporting documentation for business expense deductions for at least three years after the return is filed. The records produced by the App do not relieve you of any retention obligations imposed by tax law.
